Minions Review

By: Katerina Beaulieu

Date: February 10, 2022


Rating

I would rate Minions an 8 out of 10, just because of the difference in nature than the other movies. The whole franchise including Gru and the Minions is aimed at the same age group of audience, but it seems like the Minions movie was much more inappropriate than Despicable Me 1 or 2. There are several accounts of violence throughout the movie, even including elements of torture. Even though the movie is intended for the same audience as the previous two movies, this movie has much more violent undertones.

Recommended Audience

I would recommend Minions to anyone who enjoyed the movies Despicable Me 1 and Despicable Me 2. Even though this movie includes more violent elements than the previous two, it is very creative in giving a backstory to how the Minions came to be, and especially how they met Gru.

Trivia

    1. Director Pierre Coffin voiced all 899 minions in this film.
    2. The leading three minions were designed to resemble the three girls from the Despicable Me movies: Margo, Agnes, and Edith.
    3. Minions is the only movie with a budget under $100 million to cross the $1 billion mark worldwide, without being re-released.
