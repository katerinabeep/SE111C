Minions Recap

The Minions movie serves as a prequel to the origin of the yellow minions. Gru and his daughters are no where to be seen, and the movie has a prehistoric setting. They served many masters in the past, including a t-rex, a pharaoh, and even Napolean, yet none worked out in the end. Kevin, Bob, and Stuart will set out on a mission to find a new and fulfilling boss, which will take them to a villian convention in Florida. With the help from many along with obstacles on the way, this journey would eventually lead the minions to their current boss, Gru.

Additional information

Minions was produced by Illumination Entertainment in 2015 and distributed by Universal Pictures. The main producers were Chris Meledandri and Janet Healy, and the directors were Pierre Cofffin and Kyle Balda.
